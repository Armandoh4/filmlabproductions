After Hours

2022

- 13m

Drama

Short Film

The film follows Eva, a young American who crosses paths with two street-smart guys outside a London nightclub. What starts as a wild night out quickly spirals into something way bigger, pulling them deep into the city's gritty underworld. As they navigate danger, chaos, and questionable choices, not everyone’s guaranteed to make it out unscathed.

Shot over the course of one night in real-time, the film unfolds in chronological order, heightening the tension and urgency of their journey.
