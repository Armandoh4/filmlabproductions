Once upon a time in London

2022 - 13m

Drama

Short Film

During the week before Christmas, Sam, a street musician and performer, is struggling both mentally and financially. However, his luck seems to change when he discovers a mysterious envelope containing a suspicious amount of money and an item that leads him to question the events and decisions that led him to that moment.
