Joel M.

CEO/Director/Chief of Marketing

  • Joel Maquera is an independent screenwriter, film director, and producer from Barcelona, Spain. His passion for storytelling, photography, and visual arts led him to compete at film festivals around London and study at Greenwich University. He graduated in 2021 and founded a film production company called Filmlab Productions.
  • Maquera has written, directed, and produced several short films and adverts for clients, winning two film competitions. "Killer Santa" and "Once Upon a Time in London" premiered at the filmlocos Film Festival in 2021 and gained recognition worldwide, leading to funding for his next short film, "Kidnapped” in 2022-2023.
  • His latest project is "Ghosting," a short film in pre-production.
