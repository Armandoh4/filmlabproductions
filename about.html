About

Our Mission

Established in 2022, our mission is to revitalise entertainment.

We believe in the artistic expression of our creators, on this principle we believe politics should stay out of our films & media projects. We believe in connecting with our audience. Yes, we actually listen! So sit back and enjoy a film.

Filmlab Productions Ltd is based in the United Kingdom.
